# GSDC Minting & Acquisition Guide

This page is designed to help you navigate the process of acquiring GSDC stablecoins through our platform.

## 1. What is Minting?

**Minting** is the process of creating new digital assets (GSDC) on the blockchain. When you "mint", you exchange fiat currency (BRL) for an equivalent amount of digital tokens, backed 1:1 by our reserves.

### How it works: digital & bank flow

The minting process bridges the gap between traditional banking and decentralized finance:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create acquisition order">
    Start by initiating an acquisition order directly on the platform dashboard from the GSDC Trading section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send your BRL collateral">
    Transfer your fiat currency (BRL) to the secure bank account provided in your order instructions, including the correct Order ID reference.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Compliance and treasury verification">
    Once our treasury confirms receipt of your funds and compliance approves the order, the system triggers a **Mint** command on the blockchain.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive your GSDC">
    The system generates your new GSDC tokens and sends them directly to your registered, whitelisted wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Always double-check the destination bank details and Order ID before sending any funds.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  **Registered Wallet Verification**

  Blockchain transactions are irreversible. If GSDC is minted to an incorrect or inaccessible wallet address, the funds are permanently lost. Always verify your whitelisted wallet before confirming a mint.
</Warning>

## 2. Requirements

Before you can initiate your first acquisition request, you must complete the following mandatory setup steps (after account creation).

<Info>
  **Prerequisites**:
  - KYC-approved account  
  - Verified bank account  
  - Verified, whitelisted Web3 wallet
</Info>

### Why requirements matter

These requirements:

- Enforce AML and global regulatory compliance  
- Protect you by ensuring a clear audit trail for all transactions  
- Reduce operational and legal risk for both users and the platform  

## 3. Step-by-step acquisition flow

### 3.1 Create your acquisition order

- **Access**: Log in to your KYC-approved account.  
  _Use the same browser/device used during onboarding to avoid session issues._  
- **Start acquisition**: On the main dashboard, scroll to **GSDC Trading** and select **Acquire GSDC → Start Acquisition**.  
- **Get a quote**: Choose your **Payment Currency** (e.g. BRL), enter the desired GSDC amount, then select **Get Quote**.  
- **Review quote summary**: Confirm the indicative GSDC price, exchange rate, amount to pay in BRL, and fees.  
- **Select accounts**: Choose your whitelisted destination wallet and the verified bank account from which you will send funds, then select **Create order**.  

<Note>
  Only wallets and bank accounts that have been previously whitelisted or verified will be available for selection.
</Note>

Once submitted, the order is sent to compliance for review. After approval, you receive an email notification and can track progress.

### 3.2 Send payment and upload proof

- **Payment transfer**: Send the exact BRL amount to the banking details provided in the email or in **Show payment instructions** inside the transaction view.  
  - Cover all banking/intermediary fees yourself  
  - Include the **Order ID** in the payment memo/reference  
- **Upload proof**: Immediately upload your payment proof via **Upload Payment Proof** in the transaction view.

#### Valid payment proofs

- Official bank receipt (PDF or clear screenshot from your banking app)  
- Wire transfer confirmation with SWIFT or local transfer ID  

The proof must clearly show:

1. Sender name matching your KYC-approved name  
2. Exact amount sent (including fees)  
3. Visible **Order ID** reference  
4. Date/time within the valid quote window  

### 3.3 Rate locking, minting, and tracking

Once your payment is received and verified:

- The exchange rate is locked  
- The final GSDC amount to be minted is calculated  
- GSDC is transferred to your whitelisted wallet  

You can track all stages via:

- **Quick Actions → Transactions → Acquisition Order**  

When complete, you can download your transaction receipt from the Transactions page.

## 4. Fees

### Acquisition fee

- **Fee**: 0.15%  
- **Basis**: Applied on top of the amount to be paid for the acquired GSDC  
- **Currency**: Calculated in your chosen payment currency (e.g. BRL)  

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Example" icon="calculator">
    If you buy 10,000 BRL worth of GSDC, a 0.15% fee means you pay 10,015 BRL in total (before bank fees).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## 5. Timing and order expiry

Once your acquisition order is approved, a strict time limit applies for submitting your proof of payment.

<Warning>
  If you miss the deadline, the order expires and you must create a new acquisition request. Expired orders should not be funded.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Monitor your inbox for the order confirmation email and send your transfer + proof as soon as possible to avoid expiry.
</Tip>
