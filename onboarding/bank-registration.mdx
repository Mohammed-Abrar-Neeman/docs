# Bank Account Registration: Connecting Your Assets to GSDC

To operate within the Global South Digital Currency (GSDC) ecosystem, registering your bank account is the essential bridge between traditional finance and the blockchain.

**Pre-requisite**: Complete the **General Onboarding Process** and ensure your account is fully KYC-approved. Use the same device and browser as onboarding to avoid access issues.

## Why bank registration matters for Brazilians

For Brazilian users, registering your bank account before any transaction is critical.

### 1. Identifying your deposit during minting

When you acquire GSDC:

- Your registered Brazilian bank account acts as your unique identifier  
- Incoming BRL transfers are matched against your pre-verified bank details  
- After compliance approval, GSDC is minted and sent to your whitelisted wallet  

### 2. Receiving funds during redemption (Resgate)

When you redeem GSDC back into BRL:

- You must select **BRL (Brazilian Real)** as the redemption currency  
- Payouts can only be made to bank accounts that are verified and whitelisted  
- The account must be in your name and linked to your **CPF (Tax ID)**  

## The compliance “catch”

It is highly recommended to register your bank account via **Quick Actions → My Banks** before starting any minting or redemption order.

<Warning>
  If you add a new bank account during an ongoing order, it must go through manual compliance review. Your order cannot be processed while this review is pending.
</Warning>

Typical compliance review time during business hours is **1–2 hours**.

## Document requirements (Brazil)

To get approved on the first submission, your documents must clearly show that you are the account holder (**Pessoa Física**).

Required details:

- Full legal name identical to your KYC-approved profile  
- CPF (Tax Number) linked to the account  
- Clear Agência and Conta numbers  
- A Brazilian bank that supports BRL  

Examples of acceptable documents:

- Bank transfer proof  
- Account/debit card with name and account details  
- Internet banking screenshots showing account owner and details  

<Tip>
  Make sure screenshots are high-resolution and all critical details (name, CPF, agency, account) are clearly visible.
</Tip>

## Monitoring your registration status

You can track the status of your bank review directly in the platform:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Quick Actions">
    From the dashboard, go to **Quick Actions**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Transactions">
    Select **Transactions** to see the current status of your bank’s compliance review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email notifications">
    Watch your registered email for an approval or additional information request.
  </Step>
</Steps>
