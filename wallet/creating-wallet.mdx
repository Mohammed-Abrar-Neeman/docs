# Official Step-by-Step Guide: Creating Your MetaMask Wallet

MetaMask is a self-custodial wallet that lets you interact with the Ethereum network, where GSDC operates as an ERC-20 token. It is available as a browser extension (Chrome, Brave, Edge) and as a mobile app.

## Step 1: Download and install MetaMask

- Go to **metamask.io** (always verify the domain).  
- Install the extension for your browser or the app for iOS/Android.  

<Tip>
  Avoid unofficial app stores or links received via messages; they may be phishing attempts.
</Tip>

## Step 2: Initial setup

- Open MetaMask and click **Get Started**.  
- Select **Create a Wallet** (for a brand new wallet).  
- You can choose whether or not to share anonymous usage data with MetaMask.  

## Step 3: Create a strong password

- Choose a unique password of at least 8 characters.  
- This password only unlocks MetaMask on this device—it is not your recovery phrase.  

## Step 4: Secure your Secret Recovery Phrase

MetaMask now reveals your **Secret Recovery Phrase** (12 words).

Best practices:

- Write it down on paper (not digitally)  
- Store the paper in a safe physical location  
- Do not share it with anyone, ever  

<Warning>
  Losing this phrase or sharing it with someone means losing control of your funds.
</Warning>

## Step 5: Confirm your recovery phrase

- MetaMask will ask you to select the 12 words in the correct order.  
- This verifies that you have a valid backup.  
- After confirmation, click **All Done**.  

## Step 6: Import the GSDC token

To see your GSDC:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Tokens tab">
    On the MetaMask home screen, ensure you are on the **Ethereum Mainnet** and select the **Tokens** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import token">
    Click **Import Tokens**, then choose the **Custom Token** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add GSDC contract">
    In **Token Contract Address**, paste:  
    `0xFf5559c92d3Ee2dAF4BC7F5C2F15e6eC5a82b1e4`  
    The **GSDC** symbol and decimals should populate automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Next**, then **Import**. You should now see GSDC in your token list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 7: Add ETH for gas fees

To perform any blockchain action (including redemption):

- Acquire a small amount of **ETH** and send it to your new MetaMask address  
- Keep a buffer for future gas fees  

<Note>
  Without ETH, you will not be able to send GSDC for redemption (Resgate) or any other transaction.
</Note>
